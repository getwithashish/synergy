IRCTC

Indian Railway Catering and tourism Corporation Ltd(IRCTC)is a Mini Ratna (Category-1) Central Public Sector Enterprise under Ministry of Railways.IRCTC was incorporated on 27th September,1999 as an extended arm of the Indian Railways to upgrade ,professionalize and manage the catering and hospitality servicesat stations,on trains and other locations and to provide and to promote domestic and internationaltourism through development of budget hotels,special tour packages,information & commerce publicity and global reservation systems.The authorised capital of the company is 250 croes and paid up capital is 160crores .Its registered and Corporate office is situated at New Delhi

EVOLUTION OF INDIAN RAILWAYS


THE BEGINNING

Indian Railways began its journey on April 16, 1853, with the first train running from Bori Bunder (now Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus) in Bombay (Mumbai) to Thane, covering a distance of 34 km.

GROWTH POST INDEPENDANCE

After India gained independence in 1947, the railways underwent major expansion and modernization, unifying the fragmented railway systems inherited from the British Raj.

MODERNIZATION

Indian Railways embarked on electrification projects to enhance efficiency, reduce pollution, and increase speed. It introduced modern technologies in signaling, track construction, and train operations.

 CONTINUOUS EXPANSION

Indian Railways continues to expand its network, connecting remote areas and facilitating transportation of goods and passengers across the country, vital role in socio-economic development.
